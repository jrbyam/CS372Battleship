Modern Warship
--------------

All kinds of modern warships you need in a modern warship combat game!

The package includes 6 modern warships for US and Russian texture set.
The Russian aircraft carrier includes 2 png textures for Russian or Chinese version.
There are also submarines and destroyers included in this pack. 

The guns/turrets/hatches are separated to allow full utilisation for AI scripting.

All Warships at a glance: 

Aircraft Carriers:
- Aircraft Carrier (USA)
- Aircraft Carrier (Russian and Chinese texture)

Guns/turrets can be moved and adjusted separately.

Destroyers:
- Destroyer (USA)
- Destroyer Class (Russia)

Guns/turrets can be moved and adjusted separately.

Submarines:
- Submarine (USA)
- Submarine (Russia)

Submarine has hatches for Nuclear Missiles to open and close separately.

The models are developed for mobile games and with low polygons.
